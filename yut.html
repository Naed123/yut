Game Setup:

The game consists of 1 game board, 4 sticks, and 8 board pieces. The sticks determine the spaces each team can move while each team gets 4 of the 8 board pieces.

Gameplay:

At the start of each turn, a player from a team rolls the sticks. The player then has the option to move the amount of spaces determined by the stick roll or to introduce a new piece to the gameboard and move that piece the amount of spaces determined by the stick roll. If all four piece finish, the team wins.

Moving Options:

Players must move following the arrows along the board. Players can only change direction diagonally when the piece lands on a corner circle.

Capturing Pieces:

If one team's piece lands on another team's piece, the other teams piece becomes captured. The captured piece goes back into the team's bank and the team who captured the other team gets to roll again.(If they already rolled again for yut or mo, they do not go again)

Rolling again:

A team can roll their sticks again when they roll either 4(yut) or 5(mo).(a team can roll again only once per turn meaning rolling 4 & 4 or 5 & 5 will not let them roll again) The team can also go again when they capture another team's piece with (geol)3 or less spaces.

Stacking:

A team stack their pieces if one of their pieces land on other piece of their team. When this happens, the pieces stack will count as one, move as one, and captured as one.(even if a team decides not to stack their piece, if the other team captures the space, every piece on that space will be captured.)
